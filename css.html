Title-Education

Introduction- Education is an important tool which is very useful in everybody's life. Education is what differentiates us from other living beings on earth. It makes man the smartest creature on earth. It empowers humans and gets them ready to face challenges of life efficiently.

Types of education:

    Formal education
  • Structured and organized education that takes place in schools and other institutions
  • Includes academic studies, technical training, and professional training
  • Leads to certifications like high school diplomas or university degrees
    Informal education
  • Unstructured learning that happens in daily life, outside of schools
  • Includes learning from family, friends, work, and the media
  • Can include reading, pursuing hobbies, and learning to ride a bike
